Usage Guidelines
Display XL (52px)
Used for hero banners, landing page headlines, and major marketing sections.
Display L (44px)
Used for primary page titles and prominent section introductions.
H1 (36px)
Main page heading.
H2 (30px)
Major content section headings.
H3 (24px)
Subsection headings.
H4 (20px)
Card titles and content group headings.
H5 (18px)
Smaller content headings and form section titles.
Body Large (16px)
Default paragraph text and primary content.
Body Medium (14px)
Secondary content, descriptions, and navigation items.
Body Small (13px)
Supporting text and metadata.
Caption (12px)
Helper text, labels, and captions.
Micro Text (11px)
Legal text, disclaimers, and non-critical information only.
